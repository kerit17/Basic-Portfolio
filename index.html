About Me

Keri is a native of Northeastern Ohio where she lived with her family and attended school. After school, Keri ventured west to Albuquerque, New Mexico in search of mountains and endless blue skies. After years of missing snow, trees, and grass. Keri moved north to Denver.

Since moving to Colorado in April 2016, Keri has enjoyed all the state has to offer. Including mountain views, casual rains, and snowy slopes.
